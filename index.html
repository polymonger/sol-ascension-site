The Realm Above

A Tower Without a Ceiling

The tower has stood since before memory. No one built it. No one guards it. It simply exists, growing harder with every floor you claim. Warriors from across the realm have entered. Most stop. A few ascend. The ones who keep going discover something the others never will.

Idle Combat, Active Strategy

Your warrior fights automatically but how far they go depends on the skills you deploy, the equipment you equip, and the upgrades you invest in between floors.

Ascension Compounds Power

Each prestige run resets your floor count but keeps your ascension multiplier. The second climb is faster. The fifth is overwhelming. The tenth is unstoppable.

On-Chain, Always

Purchases go through your Solana Seed Vault, hardware-secured, non-custodial. You sign every transaction. The game never holds your assets.

Character Progression

The Path of Ascension

Floor 1

The Ascendant Awakens

Your warrior steps into the tower with nothing but raw resolve. Tap to strike. Earn gold. Upgrade damage, health, and speed, then push deeper before the enemies scale past you.

First Skills Unlock

Floor 75

Heavenly Strike & Shadow Clone

Heavenly Strike unlocks at Floor 35, calling down a divine burst that shreds elite enemies. Shadow Clone arrives at Floor 100, your mirror fights beside you, doubling every hit.

Floor 150

The Tower Remembers

Each ascension resets your floor count but your multiplier compounds permanently. Third-run warriors who've ascended twice feel it, enemies that once stalled them dissolve in seconds.

Floor 200

Transcendence

The tower's deepest floors reveal themselves only to warriors who have truly ascended. Equip the best gear, push your limits, and see what the tower has been hiding all along.

Combat System

Four Skills. One Tower.

Sol Ascension is an idle RPG but idle doesn't mean passive. Each active skill changes how your run plays out. Timing your mana orb is what separates a good run from a great one.

War Cry

Unleash a battle shout that surges your attack damage for a short window. Best used before elite enemy waves and boss floors to maximize burst damage before the window closes.

Unlocks Floor 10 High Mana Cost

Midas Touch

Turn your kills into gold. Enemies defeated while Midas Touch is active drop significantly more gold, compressing upgrade time on long runs. Pairs well with high-speed builds.

Unlocks Floor 20 Medium Mana Cost

Heavenly Strike

A divine bolt from above that deals a single massive damage hit to the current enemy. Effective against armored elites and boss-type enemies that resist sustained DPS builds.

Unlocks Floor 35 High Mana Cost Long Cooldown

Shadow Clone

Summons a mirror of your warrior to fight alongside you. The clone inherits your current attack power, effectively doubling your DPS output for the duration. The most powerful skill at peak ascension.

Unlocks Floor 100 Very High Mana Cost Very Long Cooldown

Core Gameplay

Built to Ascend

Every system in Sol Ascension is designed around a single truth: the run you're on right now is never your last.

Idle Combat

Your warrior fights continuously. No input required. Progress happens whether you're watching or not. Return to find gold waiting, floors cleared, and upgrades ready to spend.

Prestige Ascension

Ascending resets your floor count but grants a permanent power multiplier. Every ascension makes the next run faster, deeper, and stronger. There is no final ascension.

Equipment Sets

Five equipment tiers from Common through to Holy Divine. The first four tiers are completely free. The Holy Divine Set is the pinnacle tier for dedicated players.

Mana Orb System

A mana orb fills slowly over time and depletes when you use active skills. Managing when to spend mana versus when to let it rebuild is the hidden depth of Sol Ascension's combat.

18 Achievements

18 mastery badges covering kills, floors, boss defeats, gear, pets, vaults, critical hits, ascension count, leaderboard rank, and the Holy Divine Set. Progress bars on every trackable badge.

On-Chain Shop

Sol Ascension is free to play. For dedicated players, the on-chain shop offers the Holy Divine Set, with every transaction secured through your Solana Seed Vault.

Companions

The Pet System

Every pet in Sol Ascension is a face from the Solana ecosystem. Each one brings a unique passive ability that changes how you approach the climb. You can only equip one pet at a time, so choosing wisely is the difference between stalling and ascending.

BONK
BONK Token

Boosts all damage output. Bonk is the crowd favourite for pure floor progression. More damage means fewer stalls on elite floors.

+ All Damage
PUDGY
Pudgy Penguins

Amplifies critical hit damage. Best equipped when your gear already leans toward high crit chance, turning every critical hit into a devastating burst.

+ Crit Damage
FOXY
Famous Fox Federation

Increases gold dropped from every enemy. Foxy accelerates your upgrade curve. Faster gold means faster power scaling between floors.

+ Gold Drop
CLAY
Claynosaurz

Increases your overall Luck stat, which raises crit chance, improves fairy spawn rates, and increases your odds of encountering the Vault Guardian for bonus rewards.

+ Luck
MONKE
Solana Monkey Business

Adds combo hits to each attack, landing multiple strikes per tap. MONKE rewards aggressive tap-heavy playstyles with significantly higher DPS.

+ Combo Hits
JUP
Jupiter Mobile

Reduces the number of stages required to clear each floor. JUP is the efficiency pet. Ideal for ascension runs where speed of progression matters most.

- Stages Per Floor

Sol Ascension is an independent game. Pet characters are inspired by Solana ecosystem projects and are not official collaborations or endorsements.

  • shopOwned state now persists in save file, purchased items restore immediately on reload
