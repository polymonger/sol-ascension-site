The Realm Above

A Tower Without a Ceiling

The tower has stood since before memory. No one built it. No one guards it. It simply exists — and it grows harder with every floor you claim. Warriors from across the realm have entered. Most stop. A few ascend. Fewer still have seen what waits beyond Floor 200.

⚔️

Idle Combat, Active Strategy

Your warrior fights automatically — but how far they go depends on the skills you deploy, the equipment you equip, and the upgrades you invest in between floors.

🔁

Ascension Compounds Power

Each prestige run resets your floor count but keeps your ascension multiplier. The second climb is faster. The fifth is overwhelming. The tenth is unstoppable.

⛓️

On-Chain, Always

Purchases go through your Solana Seed Vault — hardware-secured, non-custodial. You sign every transaction. The game never holds your assets.

Character Progression

The Path of Ascension

Floor 1

The Ascendant Awakens

Your warrior steps into the tower with nothing but raw resolve. Tap to strike. Earn gold. Upgrade damage, health, and speed — then push deeper before the enemies scale past you.

Floor 25

First Skills Unlock

War Cry erupts at Floor 10. Midas Touch follows at Floor 20. The mana orb awakens — a resource that drains on use and fills with time. Active skill timing begins to matter.

Floor 75

Heavenly Strike & Shadow Clone

Heavenly Strike unlocks at Floor 35, calling down a divine burst that shreds elite enemies. Shadow Clone arrives at Floor 100 — your mirror fights beside you, doubling every hit.

Floor 150

The Tower Remembers

Each ascension resets your floor count but your multiplier compounds permanently. Third-run warriors who've ascended twice feel it — enemies that once stalled them dissolve in seconds.

Floor 200

Holy Divine Transcendence

The Immortal Wall stands here. No ordinary set breaks through. Equip the Holy Divine Set from the on-chain shop, return to Floor 200, and witness what the tower was hiding all along.

Combat System

Four Skills. One Tower.

Sol Ascension is an idle RPG — but idle doesn't mean passive. Each active skill changes how your run plays out. Timing your mana orb is the difference between Floor 180 and Floor 200.

⚔️

War Cry

Unleash a battle shout that surges your attack damage for a short window. Best used before elite enemy waves and boss floors to maximize burst damage before the window closes.

Unlocks Floor 10 High Mana Cost
💰

Midas Touch

Turn your kills into gold. Enemies defeated while Midas Touch is active drop significantly more gold, compressing upgrade time on long runs. Pairs well with high-speed builds.

Unlocks Floor 20 Medium Mana Cost
✨

Heavenly Strike

A divine bolt from above that deals a single massive damage hit to the current enemy. Effective against armored elites and boss-type enemies that resist sustained DPS builds.

Unlocks Floor 35 High Mana Cost Long Cooldown
🌑

Shadow Clone

Summons a mirror of your warrior to fight alongside you. The clone inherits your current attack power, effectively doubling your DPS output for the duration. The most powerful skill at peak ascension.

Unlocks Floor 100 Very High Mana Cost Very Long Cooldown

Core Gameplay

Built to Ascend

Every system in Sol Ascension is designed around a single truth: the run you're on right now is never your last.

⚔️

Idle Combat

Your warrior fights continuously — no input required. Progress happens whether you're watching or not. Return to find gold waiting, floors cleared, and upgrades ready to spend.

🔁

Prestige Ascension

Ascending resets your floor count but grants a permanent power multiplier. Every ascension makes the next run faster, deeper, and stronger. There is no final ascension.

🛡️

Equipment Sets

Five tiers from Common to Holy Divine. Each set provides scaling attack, defense, and speed bonuses. Higher tiers are purchased through the on-chain shop using SOL.

🌟

Mana Orb System

A mana orb fills slowly over time and depletes when you use active skills. Managing when to spend mana versus when to let it rebuild is the hidden depth of Sol Ascension's combat.

🏆

18 Achievements

Mastery badges track every major milestone — first ascension, Floor 200 reached, Holy Divine equipped, Shadow Clone unlocked, and more. Progress bars show exactly how close you are.

🌐

On-Chain Shop

Buy equipment sets directly with SOL. Every transaction is signed through your Solana Seed Vault. Purchases persist in your save file — they reload instantly on every session.

Patch Notes

Every build, every fix, every improvement — documented.

v0.8 Latest June 2026 ▾

Active skills system, mana orb mechanic, expanded achievements, ascension skill reset, and dApp Store submission preparation including privacy policy and terms pages.

New
  • Four active skills: War Cry (Floor 10), Midas Touch (Floor 20), Heavenly Strike (Floor 35), Shadow Clone (Floor 100)
  • Mana orb system — fills passively over time, depletes on skill activation, resets on ascension
  • Achievements expanded from 12 to 18 with progress bars on each badge
  • MASTERED tab replaces UNLOCKED for a cleaner achievement view
  • Privacy Policy page at /privacy and Terms of Use at /terms
  • Toast notification system replaces all browser alert() popups
Changed
  • Skills and mana reset fully on ascension — each run starts fresh
  • Ascension power bonus reduced from +20% to +10% per run for better long-term balance
  • Upgrade cost curve steepens faster — doubles every 4 levels instead of 5
  • LEGENDARY equipment rarity renamed to MYTHICAL for consistency
Fixed
  • Floor 190 and Floor 200 Immortal Wall warning toasts now trigger reliably
  • shopOwned state now persists in save file — purchased items restore immediately on reload
  • Tap coordinate scaling fixed on high-DPI screens using canvas bounding rect
v0.7 Major May 2026 ▾

MWA shop transactions fixed end-to-end. Domain migration to sol-ascension.com. Service worker for offline caching. Fairy event system added.

New
  • On-chain equipment shop powered by Mobile Wallet Adapter — purchase sets directly with SOL
  • sol-ascension.com domain live via Cloudflare registrar + Vercel hosting
  • Service worker (sw.js) for offline asset caching with visual progress reporting
  • TWA APK signed and submitted to Solana dApp Store
  • Fairy event system — random bonus gold drops during combat
Fixed
  • MWA signAndSendTransactions parameter corrected to payloads array (not transactions)
  • Transaction encoding fixed to standard base64 with padding (was incorrectly base64url)
  • Wallet icon path changed to relative URI — required by dApp Store submission rules

Core gameplay loop complete. Prestige ascension, equipment sets, cloud save via Upstash Redis, and global leaderboard at launch.

  • Five-tier equipment set shop — Common, Uncommon, Rare, Mythical, Holy Divine
