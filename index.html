The Game

Simple to start.
Deep to master.

Sol Ascension is a tap-based idle RPG where every run makes you permanently stronger. Defeat mobs, collect gold, upgrade your hero, and ascend — carrying your gear and pets into the next cycle at increased power. The further you push, the harder the world pushes back.

⚔
Tap Combat
Every tap deals damage. Land a critical hit and the screen shakes. Chain skills for burst windows that feel genuinely satisfying.
✦
Ascension System
Reset your floor, keep your gear and pets, and return with a permanent damage bonus. Each run is faster than the last.
◈
Gear & Crafting
Collect weapons, armor, and accessories across four rarities. Combine duplicates to craft higher-tier items. Equip what makes you strongest.
◉
Pets
Hatch six unique pets — each adds a passive bonus. BONK boosts all damage. FOXY multiplies gold. CLAY increases your crit chance.
◎
Active Skills
Four mana-powered skills unlock as you progress — War Cry, Hand of Midas, Heavenly Strike, and Shadow Clone. Use them at the right moment.
♛
Leaderboard
Compete globally. Your wallet signs your rank. Floor count and ascension count both factor in — depth rewards patience.

The Wall

Floor 200.
The Immortal Wall.

At Floor 200, mobs become unkillable. No amount of upgrades, skills, or ascensions will break through by force alone. There is only one way past.

200
Holy Divine Set
The Holy Divine Set is a permanent on-chain purchase. It shatters the Immortal Wall, transforms your hero's appearance, and unlocks Floors 200 to 1000 — environments, mob artwork, and boss types that standard players never see.
